The Four Dimensions

MBTI measures your preferences across four pairs of traits

E vs I

Extraversion / Introversion

How you gain energy: from external activities and people, or from inner reflection and solitude.

S vs N

Sensing / Intuition

How you gather information: through concrete facts and details, or through patterns and possibilities.

T vs F

Thinking / Feeling

How you make decisions: through logical analysis, or through values and how others are affected.

J vs P

Judging / Perceiving

How you approach life: with structure and planning, or with flexibility and spontaneity.
